Who we are

As a leading provider of compassionate care for senior citizens, SoftHeart Elderly Care Foundation strives to combat loneliness, improve access to healthcare, promote social engagement, and empower seniors to live with dignity, purpose, and joy in their golden years.

We provide assistance to seniors, helping them build meaningful relationships and improve the standards they are committed to. Our support includes expertise, technical trainings, and guidance in areas such as healthcare, social engagement, and environmental impacts, ensuring the highest quality of life for elderly individuals.

Our Founder

Meet Jane Nsabaa, the driving force behind SoftHEART Elderly Care Foundation. As our exceptional female leader, Jane's steadfast dedication to the cause of elderly care has illuminated the lives of numerous seniors in our community. At the heart of our mission lies the conviction that the golden years of our senior citizens should overflow with love, respect, and comfort. Under Jane's guidance, we haven't just embraced this belief; we've translated it into impactful action, ensuring that our seniors experience a life filled with care and compassion.

Madam Janet M. Nsabaa - Founder

About Us

Our overarching mission is to champion independence, dignity, and overall well-being among the elderly. This is achieved through a holistic approach that includes healthcare assistance, engaging social activities, and valuable emotional support.
